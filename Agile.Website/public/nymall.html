Huvudrubrik

The Skanska Brand Device is a dynamic extension of the Skanska Logotype. It is a symbol that confidently adapts to various contexts and formats and adds a humble, yet confident presence as a brand sender. With it´s clear and bold shapes, it tells a story of building, stability, foundation, micro, and macro perspectives and the importance of attention to detail. Our Brand Device is a set of fixed shapes, not to be altered, but adaptable in size to fit different formats, both for still and motion.
